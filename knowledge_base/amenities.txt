# Library Amenities

## Pets
- Service animals are always welcome in the library.
- Emotional support animals or other pets are not permitted inside the building. However, there is a designated waiting area outside the main doors where dogs can be tied up safely.

## Food and Drink
- Covered drinks (such as thermoses, water bottles, and coffee cups with tight-fitting lids) are permitted anywhere in the library.
- Light snacks are permitted in the main lobby and the ground floor study areas.
- No food of any kind is permitted near the public computers or in the rare book archives.

## Study Rooms
- The library offers 6 private study rooms that can be booked for up to 2 hours at a time.
- Rooms can be booked via the online portal or at the main circulation desk.
- Please be mindful of noise levels; while the rooms are sound-dampened, they are not completely soundproof.
