# Library Operating Hours and Holidays

## Standard Operating Hours
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Saturday: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed

## Holiday Closures
The library is closed on all federal holidays, including:
- New Year's Day
- Independence Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Christmas Day

## After-Hours Returns
Books can be returned after operating hours using the secure drop-box located at the main entrance. Books returned via the drop-box after closing will be processed on the next business day.
