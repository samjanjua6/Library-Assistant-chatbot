# Zylo Library — Complete Borrowing Policies & Procedures

## Borrowing Limits

- Standard members may have a maximum of 3 active loans at any given time.
- A book is considered "active" from the moment it is borrowed until it is returned.
- To borrow a 4th book, at least one currently borrowed book must be returned first.

## Loan Duration

- The standard loan period is 14 days from the date of borrowing.
- The due date is automatically calculated and displayed in your Dashboard and when requested through the AI assistant.

## Renewals

- Each loan may be renewed once, extending the due date by an additional 14 days.
- Renewals must be requested before the original due date.
- Books that are already overdue cannot be renewed.
- To renew, ask the assistant: "Renew my loan for [book title]."

## Late Fees

- A late fee of $0.50 per day is charged for each day a book is overdue.
- Late fees begin accumulating the day after the due date.
- Fees are tracked on the user's account and must be settled before new books can be borrowed.
- The maximum late fee for a single book is capped at the book's replacement cost.

## Lost Books

- A book is considered "Lost" if it has not been returned within 30 days of its due date.
- The user's account will be charged the full replacement cost of the lost book.
- An additional administrative processing fee of $10.00 will be applied for each lost book.
- Lost book charges must be cleared before the account can be used again.

## Damaged Books

- Books must be returned in the same condition as they were borrowed.
- Damage includes: water damage, torn pages, broken spines, writing/highlighting, missing covers.
- Damage assessment is made by library staff upon return.
- Minor damage: discretionary repair fee up to $5.00.
- Severe damage or complete destruction: full replacement cost plus $10.00 processing fee.

## Reserved Books

- If a book you want is currently unavailable (all copies are borrowed), you may ask the assistant to notify you when a copy becomes available.
- Reservations are fulfilled in the order they were placed.

## Inter-Library Requests

- If a specific title is not in our catalog, members may submit a purchase request.
- Contact an administrator to submit a catalog addition request.

## Account Suspension

Accounts may be suspended in the following cases:
- Outstanding late fees exceeding $20.00.
- Two or more lost book charges.
- Violation of the library's community conduct standards.

Suspended accounts lose borrowing privileges until the outstanding balance is cleared and the matter is reviewed by an administrator.

## Privacy Policy

- Member borrowing history is private and is only accessible to the member and administrators.
- Data is stored securely and never shared with third parties.
- All passwords are stored as cryptographic hashes (bcrypt) and are never recoverable.
