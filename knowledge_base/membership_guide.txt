# Zylo Library — Membership Guide

## How to Register

New members can register directly on the Zylo platform:
1. Go to the login page at mychatbot.codes/login
2. Click "Sign Up" to open the registration form
3. Enter your desired username (minimum 3 characters, maximum 50 characters)
4. Enter your email address — this must be a valid, unique email
5. Create a password (minimum 6 characters)
6. Click "Create Account" — you will be automatically logged in

Alternatively, you can sign in with your Google account using the "Continue with Google" button for a quick, password-free setup.

## Membership Tiers

### Standard Member (Default)
- Borrow up to 3 books at a time
- 14-day loan period per book
- 1 renewal per loan
- Access to the full digital catalog of 1,000+ books
- Chat with the AI Library Assistant
- Personal Dashboard with loan history

### Administrator
- All Standard Member privileges
- Access to the Admin Panel
- Manage the full book catalog (add, edit inventory)
- View all active loans across all users
- Manage user accounts and assign/revoke admin roles
- Upload documents to the AI knowledge base
- Monitor usage statistics

Administrators are appointed by existing administrators through the Admin Panel.

## Account Management

### Updating Your Profile
Currently, username and email updates must be requested from an administrator. Password changes are handled directly in your account settings.

### Deleting Your Account
To request account deletion, please speak to a library administrator.

### Linking Google Account
If you signed up with a username/password, you can still use "Continue with Google" to sign in as long as the email matches your registered email address.

## Student Membership

Students at affiliated institutions receive the same Standard membership benefits. If your institution has a partnership with Zylo Library, contact an administrator to have your account verified and upgraded.

## Library Card (Digital)

Every registered user receives a digital library card, which is their user account in the Zylo system. There is no physical card required.
