# Library Rules and Policies

## Borrowing Limits
- Each user is permitted to borrow a maximum of 3 books at any given time.
- Books can be borrowed for a maximum period of 14 days.

## Late Fees
- Late fees are applied automatically for any book that is not returned by the due date.
- The standard late fee is $0.50 per day for each overdue book.
- If a book is more than 30 days overdue, it will be considered "Lost".

## Lost or Damaged Books
- If a book is deemed Lost, the user's account will be billed for the full replacement cost of the book, plus a $10.00 administrative fee.
- Books returned with severe water damage, torn pages, or missing covers will be subject to a replacement fee at the discretion of the librarian.
