# Zylo Library — Reading Programs & Community Events

## Summer Reading Challenge

Every year from June to August, Zylo Library runs the Summer Reading Challenge. Participants track their reading and earn badges for every 5 books they complete. Top readers at the end of the summer are featured in the library's "Hall of Readers."

To join, simply register your account and start borrowing books during the summer period.

## Book Club

The Zylo Book Club meets virtually every second Saturday at 3:00 PM. Each month, a new book is selected by member vote and discussed in a group chat session. All members are welcome.

How to join:
- Register on the Zylo platform
- Express interest in the Book Club via the chat assistant
- You will be added to the next session

Current Book Club selection: Rotating monthly — ask the assistant for the current pick.

## Author Spotlight Series

Once a month, Zylo Library features a deep-dive into a specific author's catalog. The AI assistant is updated with additional knowledge about the author's biography, themes, and recommended reading order.

Past spotlights have included authors in the genres of Science Fiction, Classic Literature, and Contemporary Fiction.

## Reading Challenges by Genre

In addition to the Summer Challenge, genre-specific reading challenges run throughout the year:

- **Sci-Fi January** — Read 3 science fiction books in January
- **Mystery March** — A mystery book a week for the month of March
- **Biography May** — Explore real lives with one biography per week

Ask the assistant to recommend books for any active reading challenge.

## New Arrivals

New books are added to the catalog regularly. Ask the assistant "What are the newest books in the catalog?" to see the most recently added titles.

## Recommendations

The AI assistant can recommend books based on:
- Your past reading history
- Your preferred genre
- Books similar to ones you've enjoyed
- Bestseller and critically-acclaimed lists

Ask "Can you recommend a good thriller?" or "What should I read next if I liked Dune?"

## For Schools and Classrooms

Zylo Library partners with schools to provide classroom reading lists. Teachers can request bulk borrowing privileges for approved educational reading. Contact an administrator to set up an institutional account.
