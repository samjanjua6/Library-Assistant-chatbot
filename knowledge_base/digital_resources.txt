# Zylo Library — Digital Resources & Online Services

## Digital Catalog

Zylo Library maintains a digital catalog of over 1,000 books spanning more than 20 genres. The full catalog is searchable through the AI Library Assistant. Key genres available include:

- Classic Literature
- Science Fiction
- Fantasy
- Mystery & Thriller
- Biography & Memoir
- History
- Science & Technology
- Philosophy
- Psychology
- Self-Help & Productivity
- Romance
- Children's Books
- Young Adult
- Horror
- Travel & Adventure

## AI-Powered Catalog Search

Our AI Library Assistant can search the catalog using natural language. Examples of search queries:

- "Find me books by J.R.R. Tolkien"
- "Do you have any books about machine learning?"
- "Show me available mystery novels"
- "What Sci-Fi books do you have?"
- "Is there a copy of '1984' available?"

## Online Account Dashboard

Every registered member has access to a personal dashboard at /dashboard. From the dashboard you can:

- View your current active loans
- See due dates for each borrowed book
- Return books with a single click
- Browse featured books
- Track your reading history

## Borrowing via the AI Chat

You can borrow and return books entirely through the chat interface without ever needing to visit the library. Just tell the assistant:

- "Borrow [book title]"
- "Return [book title]"
- "What books do I currently have?"

## System Status

The Zylo Library platform runs on a cloud-hosted Azure virtual machine with 99% uptime. The AI assistant is powered by Cerebras-accelerated Llama language models, providing ultra-fast response times.
